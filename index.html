Rules:

Hit the "Rip It!" button at the tee box to see how everyone will throw their first throw. After all drives have been thrown, hit the "Rip It!" button again to see how everyone throws their second throw and repeat this until you are inside Circle 2 (60'). Once in Circle 2, hit the "Putt" button to see how everyone throws their first putt. Click "Putt" again for second putts and so on.
